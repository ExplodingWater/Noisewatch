Interactive Map

____________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Our interactive map allows you to:

  • View noise levels in any area of the city in real-time
  • Click on a location to see measurement details
  • View measurement time, noise source, and intensity in decibels
  • Read resident reports about noise in that area
  • Identify the most problematic and quietest areas of the city

Noise Reporting

____________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Anyone can contribute to noise monitoring by:

  • Audio Recording: Accurate noise measurement through your device's microphone
  • Detailed Description: Information about the source and nature of the noise
  • Precise Location: Use of GPS for accurate positioning
  • Anonymous Reporting: Protection of your privacy during reporting

The more reports we have, the more accurate our map becomes and the more effective the solutions.

Data Analysis

____________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

We provide detailed data analysis for:

  • Noise Trends: Changes in noise levels over time
  • Problem Areas: Identification of areas with the greatest issues
  • Peak Hours: When noise is highest during the day
  • Main Sources: Analysis of the most important noise sources
  • Periodic Reports: Monthly and annual data summaries
