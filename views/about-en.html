Our Mission

____________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Our mission is to create a quieter and healthier city for all residents of Tirana. We believe that information and community participation are the key to solving the problem of acoustic pollution.

Through our platform, we aim to:

  • Collect accurate data on noise levels in all areas of the city
  • Inform citizens about the impact of noise on health and quality of life
  • Create a platform where every citizen can contribute to noise monitoring
  • Provide open data for local authorities and civil society organizations
  • Help develop better urban policies for noise management
