About Noisewatch

We are a group of professionals, activists, and engaged citizens who believe that every voice matters. Noisewatch is created as a modern platform combining technology and civic awareness for a common goal: a quieter, healthier, and more livable city.

Our Mission

Our inspiration comes from the daily reality of Tirana. Our team works to provide reliable data, inform the public, and encourage active community participation.
